Media Upload & Review

Enable students to upload practice videos and audio for critique, with tools for inline feedback.

The media upload and review feature is a powerful tool for providing detailed and timely feedback. Students can upload recordings of their practice sessions, and teachers have access to a suite of tools to provide inline feedback, facilitating a rich and interactive feedback loop.

Future Capabilities

  • Inline Waveform Review: Provide feedback directly on the audio waveform.
  • Video Markup: Add comments and annotations directly to student videos.
  • AI-Based Performance Insights: Get AI-based insights into student performance.
