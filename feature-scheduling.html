Lessons & Scheduling

A powerful, flexible calendar built for music teachers, designed to automate bookings, reminders, and cancellations.

The scheduling feature will be the backbone of your studio management. We are building a system that allows you to create, view, and manage lesson times with ease, handling all the complexities of a music teacher's schedule so you don't have to.

Future Capabilities

  • Automated Rescheduling: Handle rescheduling requests automatically.
  • Conflict Detection: Prevent double-bookings and other scheduling conflicts.
  • Smart Scheduling: Suggest optimal lesson times based on availability.
