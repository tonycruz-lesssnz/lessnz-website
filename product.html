Lessnz is being built to provide a suite of powerful, integrated tools designed to streamline your workflow and amplify your teaching.

A Unified Platform for Teachers and Students

Lessnz is being designed to provide a seamless experience for both educators and learners, with distinct workflows tailored to each role.

The Teacher Experience

Lessnz will empower teachers to manage their studio with ease. From a central dashboard, you will be able to create and manage lessons, assign interactive materials, review student submissions, and track payments. The goal is to automate administrative tasks so you can focus on teaching.

The Student Experience

For students, Lessnz will provide a dedicated portal to receive weekly expectations, track their practice, upload recordings for feedback, and access a library of resources. The platform is designed to keep students engaged and motivated between lessons.

Future Vision
