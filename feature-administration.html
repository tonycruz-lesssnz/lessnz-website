Studio Administration & Automation

Automated invoicing, payment reminders, and studio analytics to help you run your studio more efficiently.

The studio administration and automation features are the cornerstone of our long-term vision. We are building a system that will handle the administrative tasks that take up so much of a teacher's time, so you can focus on what you do best: teaching.

Future Capabilities

  • Automated Invoicing: Automatically generate and send invoices to your students.
  • Automated Payment Reminders: Send automated reminders for overdue payments.
  • Studio Analytics: Track the financial health of your studio with detailed analytics.
