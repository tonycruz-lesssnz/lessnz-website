Our Roadmap

We are building Lessnz with a clear vision for the future. Here's a look at our planned development phases.

In Development

Phase 1: Foundational Platform

Our current focus is on delivering a complete, unified teaching and learning environment. This includes the core features of lesson scheduling, assignments, practice tracking, and a resource library.

Planned

Phase 2: Engagement & Workflow Intelligence

In the next phase, we will focus on increasing student accountability and teacher clarity. This will include features like a practice streak engine, assignment templates, and a lesson timeline visualization.

Planned

Phase 3: Studio OS Foundations

This phase will introduce operational stability and administrative automation. We will be building features like an automated invoice generator, smart scheduling suggestions, and teacher analytics.

Planned

Phase 4: Multi-Teacher Studios & Institutions

As we grow, we will prepare Lessnz for broader markets, including multi-teacher studios and conservatories. This will include features like a multi-teacher admin dashboard and shared resource libraries.

Planned

Phase 5: Fully Automated Studio Administration

Our long-term vision is to achieve the Lessnz "holy grail": a fully automated studio administration engine. This will include AI-generated lesson summaries, adaptive curriculum pathways, and auto-billing.
