The Story of Lessnz

It started with a simple observation: the people most passionate about teaching music were the most burdened by the tools meant to help them.

The Problem: A Symphony of Chaos

Every music educator knows the feeling. The joy of a student's breakthrough is followed by the reality of the work that comes after: scribbled lesson notes, invoices sent via email, practice reminders sent via text, resources shared via cloud links. It's a disjointed, time-consuming process that pulls focus away from what truly mattersâ€”the music.

This administrative chaos doesn't just steal time; it stifles creativity and creates a barrier between the teacher and the student. We saw brilliant educators burning out, not from teaching, but from managing.

The Spark: Where Music Met Product

Lessnz began at the intersection of two worlds. When Grammy-winning drummer and educator Kyle Swan shared his frustrations with seasoned product leader Tony Cruz, the mission became clear: build the tool they wish existed.

Our Founders

Tony Cruz

Co-Founder

A proven product leader, Tony has overseen billion-dollar software portfolios and played a key role in the successful acquisition of On Point Technology. He blends agile execution with user-centered design to ensure every solution delivers powerful, real-world impact.

Kyle Swan

Co-Founder

Grammy Award-winning drummer Kyle Swan brings over 20 years of industry mastery to Lessnz. Having toured and recorded with jazz legends like Kurt Elling, he is an active performer, adjudicator, and educator. His firsthand experience with the daily challenges of the music world provides the crucial perspective that ensures Lessnz is built by musicians, for musicians.
