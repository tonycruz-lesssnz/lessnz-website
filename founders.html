Two Founders. One Vision.
A Shared Reverence for Music, Craft, and Clarity.

Lessnz was born from the meeting of two worlds: the world of music and pedagogy, and the world of systems and product architecture.

Tony Cruz

Product & Systems

Tony is a product leader, technologist, and systems thinker who has spent his career building elegant, human-centered software. He brings clarity, structure, and technical rigor to chaos — designing systems that feel alive, adaptive, and deeply intuitive.

Kyle Swan

Music & Pedagogy

Kyle is a Grammy-winning musician, a respected private instructor, and a deeply committed educator. He saw brilliant educators spending more time organizing life than shaping musicians and wanted a system that honored the teacher’s craft.

Our Mission

To give music educators beautifully simple systems that remove administrative burden, strengthen student engagement, and preserve pedagogical integrity as we build the world’s first fully automated studio administration engine.

Our Design Principles

Clean & Academic

Interfaces should feel spacious, intentional, and free of visual noise.

Artistic Simplicity

The UI mirrors the balance of music: expressive but not chaotic.

Cognitive Flow

Every interaction should reduce friction and make the next step obvious.
