Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my free trial ends? +

After your 14-day free trial of the Pro plan, you'll be asked to add a payment method to continue using the app. If you choose not to, your account will be automatically downgraded to the Free Starter plan. You won't lose any of your core data.

Can I change my plan later? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades will take affect immediately. Downgrades will take affect at the beginning of the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. All payments are processed securely by our payment partner, Stripe.

Is my data secure? +

Yes. Data security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. You own your data, and we will never share it without your permission.
