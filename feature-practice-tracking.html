Practice Tracking & Engagement

Help students develop practice consistency and build accountability.

The practice tracking feature is a key element in fostering student engagement and motivation. Students can log their practice sessions, and teachers can gain insights into their students' progress, encouraging consistency and providing a sense of accomplishment.

Future Capabilities

  • Practice Streak Engine: Track practice streaks to motivate students and encourage daily practice.
  • Smart Reminders: Send smart reminders to help students stay on track with their practice goals.
  • Habit Coaching (AI-driven): Provide AI-driven coaching to help students develop effective practice routines.
