Resource Library

A centralized hub for all your teaching materials, including sheet music, audio, video, and more.

The resource library is a powerful tool for both teachers and students. Teachers can upload, organize, and share all their teaching materials in one place. Students have easy access to all the resources they need for their lessons and practice.

Future Capabilities

  • Categorization & Tags: Organize your resources with categories and tags for easy retrieval.
  • Preview Thumbnails: Generate preview thumbnails for all your resources.
  • Shared Libraries: Share your libraries with other teachers, creating a marketplace of resources.
